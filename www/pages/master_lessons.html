Master Level Lessons
🏫 Master Level 1 — Abstract Topics (自由, 成功)
🎯 Goals
By the end of this lesson, learners should be able to:
  • Discuss abstract concepts like freedom (自由), success (成功), happiness (幸福)
  • Use advanced adjectives and verbs for opinions and personal reflection
  • Form sentences expressing ideas, beliefs, and attitudes
📘 Vocabulary (15 words)
Chinese Pinyin English
自由zìyóufreedom
成功chénggōngsuccess
幸福xìngfúhappiness
努力nǔlìhard-working / effort
梦想mèngxiǎngdream
目标mùbiāogoal
责任zérènresponsibility
权利quánlìright
价值jiàzhívalue
尊重zūnzhòngrespect
选择xuǎnzéchoice
重要zhòngyàoimportant
机会jīhuìopportunity
改变gǎibiànchange
决定juédìngdecision
📘 Grammar Focus
1. Expressing opinions
我觉得… (I think…)
我认为… (I believe…)
对我来说… (For me…)
我觉得自由非常重要。 (I think freedom is very important.)
2. Expressing purpose or goals
为了… (in order to…)
…的目标是… (…'s goal is…)
我的目标是成功。 (My goal is success.)
为了实现梦想，我每天努力学习。 (In order to achieve my dream, I work hard every day.)
3. Using abstract nouns in complex sentences
Subject + 对 + object + 很重要 / 有意义
自由对每个人都很重要。 (Freedom is important to everyone.)
📘 Example Sentences
  • 我觉得幸福比成功更重要。 (I think happiness is more important than success.)
  • 自由是每个人的权利。 (Freedom is everyone's right.)
  • 为了实现目标，他每天努力工作。 (In order to achieve his goal, he works hard every day.)
  • 尊重别人是很重要的。 (Respecting others is very important.)
  • 我的梦想是有自己的事业。 (My dream is to have my own career.)

📘 Grammar Focus

  • 我的行李很重，需要帮助搬运。 (My luggage is heavy and I need help carrying it.)
