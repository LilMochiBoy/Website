Explorer Level Lessons
Level 1: Past & Future Time Expressions
Key Vocabulary
  • 昨天 (zuótiān) = yesterday
  • 今天 (jīntiān) = today
  • 明天 (míngtiān) = tomorrow
  • 以前 (yǐqián) = before/in the past
  • 以后 (yǐhòu) = after/in the future
  • 下个星期 (xià gè xīngqī) = next week
  • 了 (le) = past action, completion
  • 过 (guò) = past experience
  • 已经 (yǐjīng) = already
Grammar Notes
了 → marks completion of an action.
我吃饭了 = I ate.
过 → marks past experience.
我去过中国 = I have been to China.
还没… = not yet.
以前…现在… = Before… now…
Examples
  • 昨天我去学校 = I went to school yesterday.
  • 明天我会去商店 = I will go to the store tomorrow.
  • 他昨天没来 = He didn’t come yesterday.
  • 我吃过饺子 = I have eaten dumplings.
Level 2: Directions & Locations
Key Vocabulary
  • 前面 (qiánmiàn) = front
  • 后面 (hòumiàn) = back
  • 左边 (zuǒbiān) = left
  • 右边 (yòubiān) = right
  • 旁边 (pángbiān) = next to
  • 对面 (duìmiàn) = opposite
  • 中间 (zhōngjiān) = middle
  • 外面 (wàimiàn) = outside
  • 里 (lǐ) = inside
  • 近 (jìn) = near
  • 远 (yuǎn) = far
Grammar Notes
Place + 在 + Location word = “Place is at location.”
图书馆在学校旁边 = The library is next to the school.
Examples
  • 公园在饭店和超市中间 = The park is between the restaurant and the supermarket.
  • 厕所在你右边 = The toilet is on your right.
  • 我家离学校很近 = My home is close to the school.
Level 3: Daily Routines
Key Vocabulary
  • 起床 (qǐchuáng) = get up
  • 睡觉 (shuìjiào) = sleep
  • 吃饭 (chīfàn) = eat
  • 洗澡 (xǐzǎo) = shower
  • 看书 (kànshū) = read
  • 看电影 (kàn diànyǐng) = watch a movie
  • 上课 (shàngkè) = attend class
  • 运动 (yùndòng) = exercise
Grammar Notes
正在 = in the middle of doing.
一边…一边… = do two things at once.
常常 / 总是 = often / always.
Examples
  • 我每天七点起床 = I wake up at 7 every day.
  • 他正在看电视 = He is watching TV.
  • 我喜欢一边听音乐一边学习 = I like studying while listening to music.
Level 4: Transportation & Travel
Key Vocabulary
  • 公交车 (gōngjiāochē) = bus
  • 火车 (huǒchē) = train
  • 飞机 (fēijī) = airplane
  • 出租车 (chūzūchē) = taxi
  • 骑 (qí) = to ride (bike, horse)
  • 走路 (zǒulù) = to walk
  • 旅游 (lǚyóu) = to travel
  • 出发 (chūfā) = to set off
  • 票 (piào) = ticket
Grammar Notes
坐 + transport = go by…
骑 + bicycle/motorcycle = ride…
买票 = buy a ticket.
Examples
  • 我坐地铁去学校 = I go to school by subway.
  • 坐飞机比坐火车快 = Taking a plane is faster than taking a train.
  • 我昨天坐过高铁 = I have taken the high-speed train before.
Level 5: Shopping & Money
Key Vocabulary
  • 钱 (qián) = money
  • 贵 (guì) = expensive
  • 便宜 (piányi) = cheap
  • 卖 (mài) = to sell
  • 买 (mǎi) = to buy
  • 东西 (dōngxi) = things, stuff
  • 试 (shì) = to try
Grammar Notes
多少钱? = How much?
花钱 = spend money.
太…了 = too…
这双鞋太贵了 = These shoes are too expensive.
Examples
  • 这件衣服多少钱？= How much is this piece of clothing?
  • 我买了一本书 = I bought a book.
  • 我没有钱 = I don’t have money.
Level 6: Hobbies & Free Time
Key Vocabulary
  • 爱好 (àihào) = hobby
  • 打篮球 (dǎ lánqiú) = play basketball
  • 唱歌 (chànggē) = sing
  • 跳舞 (tiàowǔ) = dance
  • 游泳 (yóuyǒng) = swim
  • 上网 (shàngwǎng) = surf the internet
  • 玩游戏 (wán yóuxì) = play games
  • 周末 (zhōumò) = weekend
Grammar Notes
会 + verb = know how to…
常常 = often.
也 = also.
Examples
  • 我的爱好是看书 = My hobby is reading.
  • 她很会弹钢琴 = She is good at playing the piano.
  • 我也喜欢旅行 = I also like traveling.
Level 7: Grammar & Comparisons
Grammar Notes
比 = comparison word.
他比我高 = He is taller than me.
更 = more.
最 = most.
一样 = the same.
Examples
  • 今天比昨天冷 = Today is colder than yesterday.
  • 中文比英文难 = Chinese is harder than English.
  • 她和我一样高 = She is as tall as me.
  • 他是我们班最聪明的人 = He is the smartest in our class.
Level 8: Complex Sentences
Key Words
  • 因为…所以… = because… therefore…
  • 如果… = if…
  • 但是 = but
  • 虽然…但是… = although… but…
  • 先…然后… = first… then…
Examples
  • 因为下雨，所以我没去学校 = Because it rained, I didn’t go to school.
  • 如果明天不下雨，我们去爬山 = If it doesn’t rain tomorrow, we will go hiking.
  • 我先吃饭，然后写作业 = I eat first, then do homework.
Level 9: Culture & Festivals
Key Vocabulary
  • 春节 (Chūnjié) = Spring Festival (Chinese New Year)
  • 中秋节 (Zhōngqiū Jié) = Mid-Autumn Festival
  • 端午节 (Duānwǔ Jié) = Dragon Boat Festival
  • 饺子 (jiǎozi) = dumplings
  • 月饼 (yuèbǐng) = mooncake
  • 红包 (hóngbāo) = red envelope
  • 灯笼 (dēnglóng) = lantern
  • 节日 (jiérì) = festival
Examples
  • 春节是中国最重要的节日 = Spring Festival is the most important festival in China.
  • 中秋节人们常常吃月饼 = People often eat mooncakes during the Mid-Autumn Festival.
  • 他们给孩子红包 = They give children red envelopes.
Level 10: Self-Expression & Daily Life
Key Vocabulary
  • 将来 (jiānglái) = in the future
  • 健康 (jiànkāng) = health
  • 生气 (shēngqì) = angry
  • 开心 (kāixīn) = happy
Examples
  • 我将来想当医生 = I want to be a doctor in the future.
  • 我觉得学习中文很有用 = I think learning Chinese is very useful.
  • 你以后想做什么工作？= What job do you want to do in the future?
  • 我每天都用手机学习 = I use my phone to study every day.
