Master Level Lessons
Level 1: Advanced Expressions & Comprehension
Key Vocabulary
  • 无论 (wúlùn) = no matter
  • 尽力而为 (jìnlì ér wéi) = to do one’s best
  • 哪怕 (nǎpà) = even if / even though
  • 流利 (liúlì) = fluent
  • 充满希望 (chōngmǎn xīwàng) = full of hope
  • 挑战 (tiǎozhàn) = challenge
  • 意义 (yìyì) = meaning, significance
  • 坚持 (jiānchí) = to persist, to keep going
  • 自大 (zìdà) = arrogant
  • 欣赏 (xīnshǎng) = to appreciate, admire
Idioms
  • 化险为夷 = turn danger into safety
  • 杯弓蛇影 = false alarm, imagining danger
  • 掩耳盗铃 = to deceive oneself
  • 守株待兔 = wait idly for opportunity
Grammar Notes
  • 无论…都… = no matter… still…
  • 哪怕…也… = even if… still…
  • 充满 + noun = full of…
无论结果如何，我们都应该尽力而为 = No matter the result, we should do our best.
哪怕下雨，我也要去 = Even if it rains, I will go.
他在北京生活了十年，汉语已经非常流利 = He has lived in Beijing for ten years, his Chinese is very fluent.
他对未来充满了希望 = He is full of hope for the future.
这次经历让我明白了团队合作的重要性 = This experience made me realize the importance of teamwork.
他总是很自大，让别人不太舒服 = He is always arrogant, which makes others uncomfortable.
用4–5句话写一段你经历过的难忘的挑战。
(Describe what happened, how you felt, and what you learned.)
Level 2: Dedication & Kindness
Key Vocabulary
  • 不但…而且 (bùdàn… érqiě) = not only… but also
  • 取消 (qǔxiāo) = cancel
  • 勤奋 (qínfèn) = diligent
  • 善良 (shànliáng) = kind
  • 共同 (gòngtóng) = together
  • 升职 (shēngzhí) = promotion
  • 挑战 (tiǎozhàn) = challenge
Idioms
  • 不堪入目 = too ugly to look at
  • 不值一提 = not worth mentioning
  • 塞翁失马 = a blessing in disguise
Grammar Notes
  • 不但…而且… = not only… but also…
  • 如果…就… = if… then…
他不但聪明，而且非常勤奋 = He is not only smart but also hardworking.
如果明天下雨，我们就取消计划 = If it rains tomorrow, we will cancel the plan.
他很善良，总是关心别人 = He is kind and always cares for others.
塞翁失马，焉知非福 = Misfortune may turn out to be a blessing.
写5–6句话：介绍你文化中的一个重要传统。

  • 归根到底 (guīgēn dàodǐ) = ultimately
  • 影响 (yǐngxiǎng) = influence

Idioms

Grammar Notes

他被迫接受了这个条件 = He was forced to accept the condition.
科学家正在努力发明新的能源解决方案 = Scientists are working to invent new energy solutions.

Level 9: Integrity & Social Issues
Key Vocabulary
  • 宁可…也不… = would rather… than…
  • 诚信 (chéngxìn) = honesty
  • 勇敢 (yǒnggǎn) = brave
  • 庞大 (pángdà) = huge
  • 责任 (zérèn) = responsibility
Idioms
  • 胸有成竹 = confident, well-prepared
Grammar Notes
  • 为了 + purpose = for the sake of…
  • 减少… = reduce…
他认为诚信比金钱更重要 = He thinks honesty is more important than money.
我宁可自己辛苦，也不麻烦别人 = I’d rather suffer myself than trouble others.
为了保护环境，我们应该减少使用塑料袋 = To protect the environment, reduce plastic use.
全球化既有优点，也有缺点 = Globalization has both pros and cons.
写6–7句话：谈谈年轻人在社会未来发展中的作用。
Level 10: Vision & Humanity
Key Vocabulary
  • 无论…都… = no matter… still…
  • 关键 (guānjiàn) = key, crucial
  • 坚持 (jiānchí) = persist
  • 失败 (shībài) = failure
  • 博大精深 (bódà jīngshēn) = broad and profound
Grammar Notes
  • 之所以…是因为… = the reason why… is because…
无论天气如何，我们都要完成任务 = No matter the weather, we must finish the task.
他克服了许多困难，终于成功了 = He overcame difficulties and finally succeeded.
他之所以成功，是因为他从不放弃 = He succeeded because he never gave up.
只有合作，才能解决全球挑战 = Only cooperation can solve global challenges.
写8–10句话：描绘你对人类未来的愿景，包括科技、文化和合作。
