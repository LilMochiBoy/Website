Challenger Level Lessons
Level 1: Advanced Grammar & Environmental Issues
Concessive Structures
  • 尽管 = although / even though
  • 不管 = no matter (what/how)
尽管下雨，他还是去跑步。
(Although it rained, he still went running.)
不管天气怎么样，他每天坚持跑步。
(No matter the weather, he insists on running every day.)
Verb Completion Words
  • 完成 = to complete (a task/requirement)
  • 结束 = to finish, to end (meeting/event)
  • 完结 = to conclude (formal, literary)
  • 完了 = colloquial “it’s over”
Measure Words
  • 知识 → 项 (一项知识 = one item/piece of knowledge)
Useful Vocabulary
  • 复杂 (complex) → synonyms: 繁琐 (tedious), 困难 (difficult)
  • 感动 = moved, touched emotionally
  • 空气污染 = air pollution
  • 环境保护 = environmental protection
Idioms
  • 一石二鸟 = kill two birds with one stone
Practice Tip: Focus on writing 2–3 short sentences using 尽管 / 不管 + clause.
Level 2: Hopes, Challenges & Formal Language
Expressions of Hope & Encouragement
我希望以后能出国留学。
(I hope to study abroad in the future.)
老师鼓励我们勇敢地提问题。
(The teacher encouraged us to ask questions bravely.)
Opposites & Word Choice
  • 乐观 (optimistic) ↔ 悲观 (pessimistic)
  • 高效 = efficient (most natural choice)
Formal vs. Informal
  • 我饿了。 (informal)
  • 我需要用餐。 (formal)
Vocabulary
  • 挑战 = challenge
  • 便利 = convenience
  • 任务 = task
Practice Tip: Try writing one formal and one casual version of the same idea.
Level 3: Perseverance & Education
坚持 (to persist)
  • 坚持 → synonyms: 坚决, 毅力
尽管失败了，他还是坚持不放弃。
Useful Words
  • 冷静 = calm
  • 只 = only
  • 严格 = strict
  • 压力 = pressure
Idioms
  • 开夜车 = burning the midnight oil
Practice Tip: Write a paragraph using 坚持 + 不放弃.
Level 4: Formal Work & Negotiations
被选为… (to be elected as…)
他被选为学生会主席。
Vocabulary
  • 负责 = responsible
  • 有价值的 = valuable
  • 达成协议 = to reach an agreement
  • 能力 = ability
  • 支持 = support
Idioms
  • 罗马不是一天建成的 = Rome wasn’t built in a day
Practice Tip: Write a mock application letter using 负责, 能力, 支持.
Level 5: Persistence & Responsibility
Expressions

我从来没有迟到过。
Vocabulary

  • 节日 = festival
  • 责任 = responsibility
  • 发展 = development
Idioms

Practice Tip: Write 2 sentences each with 无论…都… and 从来没有…过.
