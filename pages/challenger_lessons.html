Challenger Level Lessons
🏫 Challenger Level 1 — Hobbies & Interests (再深入)
🎯 Goals
By the end of this lesson, learners should be able to:
  • Talk about hobbies and interests in more detail.
  • Ask and answer "What do you like to do?" with additional nuance.
  • Use verbs like 喜欢, 爱, 常常, 有时候.
📘 Vocabulary (15 words)
ChinesePinyinEnglish
喜欢xǐhuānlike
爱àilove
常常chángchángoften
有时候yǒushíhòusometimes
玩游戏wán yóuxìplay games
看书kàn shūread books
听音乐tīng yīnyuèlisten to music
唱歌chàng gēsing
跳舞tiào wǔdance
打篮球dǎ lánqiúplay basketball
打羽毛球dǎ yǔmáoqiúplay badminton
游泳yóuyǒngswim
运动yùndòngexercise / sport
喜剧xǐjùcomedy
科幻kēhuànsci-fi
📘 Grammar Focus
1. Expressing frequency
常常 + verb → often do something
我常常听音乐。 (I often listen to music.)
有时候 + verb → sometimes do something
我有时候打篮球。 (I sometimes play basketball.)
2. Expressing love / strong liking
爱 + verb → love doing something
我爱游泳。 (I love swimming.)
3. Asking about hobbies
你喜欢做什么？ (What do you like to do?)
你爱运动吗？ (Do you love sports?)
4. Talking about preferences for types
我喜欢看喜剧电影。 (I like watching comedy movies.)
他常常听科幻音乐。 (He often listens to sci-fi music.)
📘 Example Sentences
  • 我常常看书。 (I often read books.)
  • 她有时候唱歌。 (She sometimes sings.)
  • 他爱打篮球。 (He loves playing basketball.)
  • 你喜欢做什么？ (What do you like to do?)
  • 我喜欢听音乐。 (I like listening to music.)
  • 我爱游泳。 (I love swimming.)
  • 我们常常一起玩游戏。 (We often play games together.)
  • 他喜欢看喜剧电影。 (He likes watching comedy movies.)
🏫 Challenger Level 2 — Travel (买票, 订酒店)
🎯 Goals
By the end of this lesson, learners should be able to:
  • Book tickets and hotels in Chinese.
  • Ask and answer questions about transportation and accommodation.
  • Use polite phrases and quantities in travel situations.
📘 Vocabulary (15 words)
ChinesePinyinEnglish
买票mǎi piàobuy ticket
火车票huǒchē piàotrain ticket
飞机票fēijī piàoairplane ticket
订酒店dìng jiǔdiànbook hotel
旅馆lǚguǎnhotel / inn
房间fángjiānroom
单人间dān rén jiānsingle room
双人间shuāng rén jiāndouble room
多少钱duōshao qiánhow much
票piàoticket
去qùgo to
从cóngfrom
到dàoto
请问qǐng wènexcuse me / may I ask
可以kěyǐcan / may
📘 Grammar Focus
1. Asking for tickets
我要 + ticket type → I want …
我要一张火车票。 (I want a train ticket.)
从…到… → from … to …
我要从北京到上海的火车票。 (I want a train ticket from Beijing to Shanghai.)
2. Booking hotel rooms
订 + room type → book a … room
我要订一个双人间。 (I want to book a double room.)
多少钱？ → how much?
这个房间多少钱？ (How much is this room?)
3. Polite expressions
请问 + question → Excuse me, may I ask …
请问，去火车站怎么走？ (Excuse me, how do I get to the train station?)
可以 + verb → Can I … / Is it possible …
我可以订酒店吗？ (Can I book a hotel?)
📘 Example Sentences
  • 我要买一张火车票。 (I want to buy a train ticket.)
  • 我要从北京到上海的飞机票。 (I want an airplane ticket from Beijing to Shanghai.)
  • 我要订一个单人间。 (I want to book a single room.)
  • 请问，这个房间多少钱？ (Excuse me, how much is this room?)
  • 我可以订旅馆吗？ (Can I book a hotel?)
  • 我要两张票。 (I want two tickets.)
  • 旅馆离车站远吗？ (Is the hotel far from the station?)
  • 我想去广州。 (I want to go to Guangzhou.)

🎯 Goals
By the end of this lesson, learners should be able to:
  • Talk about professions, workplaces, and daily work routines.
  • Ask and answer questions about jobs and responsibilities.
  • Use time expressions to describe working hours.
📘 Vocabulary (15 words)

📘 Grammar Focus
1. Talking about work
工作 + 职业 → to describe a profession
我是老师。 (I am a teacher.)
在 + 公司 / 办公室 → location of work
我在公司工作。 (I work at a company.)
2. Talking about daily routines
每天 + time + verb → everyday action
我每天早上八点上班。 (I go to work at 8 am every day.)
上班 / 下班 → go to work / finish work
我早上八点上班，下午五点下班。 (I start work at 8 am and finish at 5 pm.)
3. Asking about work
你做什么工作？ (What is your job?)
你每天几点上班？ (What time do you go to work every day?)
4. Talking about colleagues and meetings
我和同事开会。 (I have a meeting with colleagues.)
老板要我写报告。 (The boss asked me to write a report.)
📘 Example Sentences
  • 我是医生。 (I am a doctor.)
  • 他在公司工作。 (He works at a company.)
  • 我每天早上八点上班。 (I go to work at 8 am every day.)
  • 下午五点下班。 (I finish work at 5 pm.)
  • 我和同事开会。 (I have a meeting with colleagues.)
  • 老板要我写报告。 (The boss wants me to write a report.)
  • 我周末放假。 (I have holidays on weekends.)
  • 昨天我加班到晚上十点。 (I worked overtime until 10 pm yesterday.)
