Rookie Level Lessons
Level 1: First Steps (Greetings, Pinyin, Tones)
Key Vocabulary
  • 你好 (nǐ hǎo) = Hello
  • 谢谢 (xièxie) = Thank you
  • 再见 (zàijiàn) = Goodbye
  • 对不起 (duìbuqǐ) = Sorry
  • 请 (qǐng) = Please
  • 没关系 (méi guānxi) = It’s okay
  • 老师 (lǎoshī) = Teacher
  • 学生 (xuéshēng) = Student
Pinyin Tones
  • mā (妈 – mother) → First tone: flat, high
  • má (麻 – hemp) → Second tone: rising
  • mǎ (马 – horse) → Third tone: falling-rising
  • mà (骂 – scold) → Fourth tone: falling
Tone marks:
  • ˉ = First tone
  • ˊ = Second tone (rising)
  • ˇ = Third tone
  • ˋ = Fourth tone
Examples
Q: 你好吗？ (nǐ hǎo ma?) = How are you?
A: 我很好 (wǒ hěn hǎo) = I’m fine.
Greeting teacher: 老师好 (lǎoshī hǎo).
您 (nín) = polite “you.”
Practice
Write pinyin: 谢谢 → xièxie
对不起 → 没关系 (sorry → it’s okay)
中国 → Zhōngguó (correct spelling).
Level 2: Numbers & Days
Numbers
  • 一 (yī) = 1
  • 二 (èr) = 2
  • 三 (sān) = 3
  • 六 (liù) = 6
  • 七 (qī) = 7
  • 八 (bā) = 8
  • 九 (jiǔ) = 9
  • 十 (shí) = 10
  • 十一 (shíyī) = 11
  • 十二 (shí’èr) = 12
Days
  • 今天 (jīntiān) = today
  • 明天 (míngtiān) = tomorrow
  • 昨天 (zuótiān) = yesterday
  • 星期 (xīngqī) = week, + number for day (星期一 = Monday)
  • 星期天 / 星期日 = Sunday
Dates
Word order: Year – Month – Day
Example: 2025年9月6日 → September 6, 2025
Level 3: Pronouns & Simple Sentences
Pronouns
  • 我 (wǒ) = I, me
  • 你 (nǐ) = you
  • 您 (nín) = polite you
  • 他 (tā) = he
  • 她 (tā) = she
  • 它 (tā) = it
  • 我们 (wǒmen) = we, us
  • 你们 (nǐmen) = you (plural)
  • 他们 / 她们 (tāmen) = they
Examples
我是学生 = I am a student.
我们是朋友 = We are friends.
她是我的姐姐 = She is my older sister.
我不是老师，我是学生 = I’m not a teacher, I’m a student.
Level 4: Family & People
Family Words
  • 爸爸 (bàba) = father
  • 妈妈 (māma) = mother
  • 哥哥 (gēge) = older brother
  • 姐姐 (jiějie) = older sister
  • 弟弟 (dìdi) = younger brother
  • 妹妹 (mèimei) = younger sister
  • 爷爷 (yéye) = grandfather (father’s side)
  • 孩子 (háizi) = child
  • 家 (jiā) = family, home
Examples
这是我的朋友 = This is my friend.
她有一个弟弟 = She has a younger brother.
我爱我的家 = I love my family.
他是我妈妈的爸爸，他是我的爷爷 = He is my mother’s father, he is my grandfather.

Examples

我喜欢喝茶 = I like to drink tea.
我吃面包 = I eat bread.
Level 6: Food & Drinks
Vocabulary
  • 饺子 (jiǎozi) = dumplings
  • 商店 (shāngdiàn) = shop
  • 医院 (yīyuàn) = hospital
  • 银行 (yínháng) = bank
  • 公园 (gōngyuán) = park
  • 饭店 (fàndiàn) = restaurant
  • 咖啡 (kāfēi) = coffee
  • 菜 (cài) = dish, vegetables
  • 鸡蛋 (jīdàn) = egg
  • 面条 (miàntiáo) = noodles
Examples
我们去饭店 = We are going to the restaurant.
你喝水吗？ = Do you drink water?
我在商店买苹果 = I buy apples at the store.
一碗面条 = a bowl of noodles.
Level 7: Measure Words
Common Measure Words
  • 个 (gè) = general measure word (most common)
  • 本 (běn) = books
  • 只 (zhī) = animals, one of a pair
  • 条 (tiáo) = long, thin things (fish, pants, roads)
  • 辆 (liàng) = vehicles
  • 杯 (bēi) = cup/glass
Examples
一本书 = one book
一杯茶 = a cup of tea
他有三只狗 = He has three dogs.
两辆车 = two cars
Level 8: Time & Weather
Vocabulary
  • 现在 (xiànzài) = now
  • 点 (diǎn) = o’clock
  • 今天 (jīntiān) = today
  • 昨天 (zuótiān) = yesterday
  • 明天 (míngtiān) = tomorrow
  • 冷 (lěng) = cold
  • 热 (rè) = hot
  • 下雨 (xiàyǔ) = to rain
  • 雪 (xuě) = snow
  • 风 (fēng) = wind
Examples
现在几点？ = What time is it now?
今天很冷 = Today is cold.
明天有太阳 = Tomorrow there will be sun.
今天刮风 = It is windy today.

Level 10: Short Self-Expression
Useful Sentences

  • 我的爱好是音乐 = My hobby is music.
  • 我在学中文 = I am learning Chinese.
  • 我喜欢看书 = I like reading.
  • 我喜欢中国文化 = I like Chinese culture.
