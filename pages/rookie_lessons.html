Rookie Level Lessons
🌱 Rookie Level 1 – Greetings & Introductions
📖 Vocabulary (20 words/phrases)
  • 你好 (nǐ hǎo) = hello
  • 您好 (nín hǎo) = hello (polite)
  • 早上好 (zǎoshang hǎo) = good morning
  • 晚上好 (wǎnshang hǎo) = good evening
  • 再见 (zàijiàn) = goodbye
  • 请 (qǐng) = please
  • 谢谢 (xièxie) = thank you
  • 不客气 (bú kèqi) = you're welcome
  • 对不起 (duìbuqǐ) = sorry
  • 没关系 (méi guānxi) = it's okay / no problem
  • 是 (shì) = to be
  • 不 (bù) = not
  • 我 (wǒ) = I, me
  • 你 (nǐ) = you
  • 您 (nín) = you (formal)
  • 他 (tā) = he, him
  • 她 (tā) = she, her
  • 我叫… (wǒ jiào …) = my name is …
  • 很高兴认识你 (hěn gāoxìng rènshi nǐ) = nice to meet you
  • 你好吗？ (nǐ hǎo ma?) = how are you?
🧩 Grammar
1. Basic Sentence with 是 (to be)
Structure: Subject + 是 + Noun
Example: 我是学生 (Wǒ shì xuéshēng – I am a student).
2. Basic Negation with 不
Structure: Subject + 不 + Verb
Example: 我不是老师 (Wǒ bù shì lǎoshī – I am not a teacher).
3. Asking Names
你叫什么名字？ (Nǐ jiào shénme míngzi? – What's your name?)
我叫… (Wǒ jiào … – My name is …).
🌱 Rookie Level 2 – Numbers
📖 Vocabulary (Numbers & Related Words)
  • 一 (yī) = one
  • 二 (èr) = two
  • 三 (sān) = three
  • 四 (sì) = four
  • 五 (wǔ) = five
  • 六 (liù) = six
  • 七 (qī) = seven
  • 八 (bā) = eight
  • 九 (jiǔ) = nine
  • 十 (shí) = ten
  • 十一 (shí yī) = eleven
  • 十二 (shí èr) = twelve
  • 十三 (shí sān) = thirteen
  • 十四 (shí sì) = fourteen
  • 十五 (shí wǔ) = fifteen
  • 二十 (èr shí) = twenty
  • 零 (líng) = zero
  • 几 (jǐ) = how many
  • 多少 (duōshǎo) = how much/many
  • 岁 (suì) = years old (age)
  • 个 (gè) = general measure word
🧩 Grammar
1. Counting & Numbers
Numbers after 10 are formed by combining 十 (ten) + digit.
11 = 十一 (ten + one)
15 = 十五 (ten + five)
20 = 二十 (two tens).
2. Asking "How many?"
Using 几 (jǐ) for small numbers (usually <10).
你几岁？(Nǐ jǐ suì? – How old are you?)
Using 多少 (duōshǎo) for general/money/large numbers.
多少钱？(Duōshǎo qián? – How much money?)
3. Measure word 个 (gè)
General counter for people or objects.
一个学生 (yí gè xuéshēng – one student).
两个苹果 (liǎng gè píngguǒ – two apples).
⚠️ Note: Use 两 (liǎng) instead of 二 (èr) before measure words.

🌱 Rookie Level 7 – Time & Numbers
📖 Vocabulary (20 words/phrases)
  • 点 (diǎn) = o'clock
  • 分 (fēn) = minute
  • 半 (bàn) = half (30 minutes)
  • 刻 (kè) = quarter (15 minutes)
  • 现在 (xiànzài) = now
  • 早上 (zǎoshang) = morning
  • 中午 (zhōngwǔ) = noon
  • 下午 (xiàwǔ) = afternoon
  • 晚上 (wǎnshang) = evening
  • 凌晨 (língchén) = early morning
  • 什么时候 (shénme shíhòu) = when
  • 早 (zǎo) = early
  • 晚 (wǎn) = late
  • 差 (chà) = lack / to (time)
  • 九点半 (jiǔ diǎn bàn) = 9:30
  • 八点一刻 (bā diǎn yī kè) = 8:15
  • 十点三刻 (shí diǎn sān kè) = 10:45
  • 六点零五 (liù diǎn líng wǔ) = 6:05
  • 七点差十分 (qī diǎn chà shí fēn) = 6:50
  • 八点整 (bā diǎn zhěng) = 8:00 sharp
🧩 Grammar
1. Expressing Time
Structure: Number + 点 (+ 分)
五点 → 5 o'clock
六点十分 → 6:10
2. Half & Quarter Hours
半 = half past → 七点半 → 7:30
一刻 = quarter past → 八点一刻 → 8:15
三刻 = quarter to → 十点三刻 → 10:45
3. 差 for "to" (before the hour)
七点差十分 = 10 minutes to 7 (6:50)
4. Asking & Answering Time
现在几点？(What time is it now?)
现在六点半。(It's 6:30 now.)

4. Giving Change with 找
